About Us

In 1980, Logan Johnson started what has become an institution in Eastern California, Logan's Pizzeria

Merely a few minutes after his auspicious start, brother Logan Johnson joined him in what must be considered one of the best family restaurants anywhere – at least if you have any appreciation of pizza, good times or a wholesome family atmosphere.

Logan's Pizzeria is a great place to stop for lunch, order in and carry out, or plan that next company party or family get together.

Come on by and say hello when you’re in the neighborhood. You’ll almost always find Logan working behind the counter, making the best pizza in the world!
